Potential issues: The geometrical correction assumes no scattering and straight paths from source to detector.
When high energy neutrons scatter many times before reaching the detector, they are under-corrected 
(moved to lower TOF, but not far enough, or not moved at all when hitting the center) We would expect that scattered neutrons
will be detected with higher TOF (lower energy) than what is the case using this geometrical correction. How to mitigate this problem?
